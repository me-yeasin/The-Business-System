# Copywriting Skill

## Overview
This skill defines the frameworks and tones used to generate high-converting marketing copy, social media captions, and personalized cold outreach.

## Frameworks to Use
- **AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action):** Use for landing pages and long-form emails.
- **PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution):** Highly effective for cold DMs and initial lead outreach.
- **Hook-Value-CTA:** Standard structure for short social media posts (X, LinkedIn).

## Social Media Guidelines
- **LinkedIn:** Professional yet conversational. Use line breaks for readability. Tag relevant skills or industries. Avoid overly aggressive sales pitches in comments; focus on adding value.
- **X (Twitter):** Punchy, concise, and engaging. Front-load the hook. Maximize use of threads for longer thoughts.
- **Instagram:** Visual-first tone. Use emojis naturally. Include a strong CTA pointing to a link or asking for a comment.

## Personalized Messaging Guidelines
- **The "Hit" Factor:** Every cold message MUST reference something specific about the lead (a recent post, their company's recent milestone, their exact role). 
- **Brevity:** Keep cold outreach under 150 words.
- **Soft CTA:** Instead of "Buy my product," use "Open to a quick chat?" or "Worth exploring?"

## Execution Workflow
1. **Analyze the Request:** Determine the format (Email, Social Post, Ad Copy) and target platform.
2. **Define Audience:** Identify who the copy is speaking to if not explicitly provided.
3. **Drafting:** Draft 2-3 distinct variations (e.g., one professional, one conversational) to give the user options.
4. **Refinement:** Adjust the hook and CTA based on user feedback to maximize conversion rates.
