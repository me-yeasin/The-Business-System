# Cross-Platform Strategy: AI Websites & Next.js Performance

## 1. Platform-Specific Hooks & Formatting
The "Trojan Horse" Angle: We show them how easy it is to build an AI website, but then expose its critical flaw—poor performance (The "Performance Gap")—which kills their ad spend.

### LinkedIn
* **Format:** Text-heavy storytelling post with a short video and a PDF Carousel breakdown.
* **Hook:** *"I had an AI build an e-commerce site in 10 minutes. Then I ran it through Google PageSpeed Insights. It scored a 42. Here's why AI websites are burning your ad budget."*

### X (Twitter)
* **Format:** High-value thread with a punchy lead tweet and a 60-second summary video.
* **Hook:** *"AI website builders get you to 80% in minutes. But that last 20%? It's costing you 40% of your sales. A breakdown of bloated AI sites vs. custom headless Next.js:"*

### Instagram Reels
* **Format:** Fast-paced screen recording with high-contrast captions.
* **Hook:** *"Watch AI build a store in 60 seconds... and watch it fail the 2.5-second load test."* (Show the over-the-shoulder PageSpeed Insights test).

### TikTok
* **Format:** Raw, direct-to-camera, slightly controversial.
* **Hook:** *"If your Shopify store takes more than 2.5 seconds to load, you are giving your competitors money. Let's see if this AI store survives the test."*

### YouTube Shorts
* **Format:** Search-driven, loopable micro-tutorial.
* **Hook:** *"AI Website Builders vs. Google Core Web Vitals. Who wins? (Spoiler: Not your conversion rate)."*

## 2. Repurposing Strategy
* **Chop & React:** Take the exact 15-second moment the AI site fails the PageSpeed test and use it as your high-viral top-of-funnel clip for TikTok and Reels.
* **Distill into Frameworks:** Turn our "6-Step Gauntlet" into a LinkedIn Carousel: *"6 Checks Every E-com Owner Must Pass Before Scaling Ad Spend."*
* **SEO Blog Post:** Write an optimized blog post targeting "AI website builder vs Next.js performance", ensuring the primary keyword is in the H1.

## 3. CTA Mapping (Driving Leads to the Next.js Offer)
* **Soft CTA (Top of Funnel):** *"Comment 'SPEED' and I'll send you the exact prompt I used to build this AI site, plus my Core Web Vitals checklist."*
* **Hard CTA (Mid/Bottom Funnel):** *"Are you currently running ads to a site that loads slower than 2.5s? Drop your URL below or DM me. I'll run it through our Performance Gauntlet. If your mobile score is <60, I'll show you exactly how a Next.js migration will boost your ROI."*
