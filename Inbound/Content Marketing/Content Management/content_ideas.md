# 5 High-Priority Content Ideas (Based on Real Forum Data)

These 5 content ideas were generated through a deep-dive into real community forums (like Predpriemach) and are based on exact, verbatim complaints from Bulgarian e-commerce owners.

### 1. The SaaS Inventory Sync Nightmare (Cloudcart)
*   **The Real Evidence:** *"от 2 години е така и почти всяка седмица мънкаме на някой че нещо е изчерпано а в магазина се водят бройки... това ми бърка работата"*
*   **The Content Idea:** **The Hidden Cost of "Out of Stock" Errors in SaaS.** Address the headache of delayed inventory syncs on hosted platforms forcing owners to apologize to angry customers.
*   **The Pitch:** Pitch Next.js/Headless connecting directly to warehouse ERP via webhooks for 100% real-time inventory. *"Tired of apologizing for inventory mismatches? Let's audit your architecture."*

### 2. The "Custom Feature" Ransom (SaaS Platforms)
*   **The Real Evidence:** *"Не ги препоръчвам... Ако искаш нещо къстъм си приготви пари и то доста, режат глави! Мой познат... цената която му изпляскаха беше 16 к. лева."*
*   **The Content Idea:** **When SaaS Costs More Than Custom.** Debunk the "cheap" SaaS myth. Expose how hosted platforms hold businesses hostage with ridiculous quotes when they need a custom feature.
*   **The Pitch:** Compare Total Cost of Ownership. With Next.js, they own the code and aren't held for ransom. *"Don't get held for ransom by your SaaS provider. Book a discovery call."*

### 3. The WooCommerce Speed Wall
*   **The Real Evidence:** *"Защо woocommerce е толкова бавен? ... инсталирах си воокомерс ... без много снимки... получавам мобиле 10-15... инсталирах разни кеш плъгин ... и няма мърдане"*
*   **The Content Idea:** **Why Caching Plugins Won't Fix Your WooCommerce Speed Score.** Break down why traditional WordPress architecture hits a speed wall that no caching plugin can fix.
*   **The Pitch:** Introduce Next.js decoupled frontend (keep WP backend but get instant-loading site). *"Stuck at 15/100 on Mobile? Send me your link for a Free Next.js Performance Mockup."*

### 4. The SaaS SEO Illusion ("A Stall in a Mall")
*   **The Real Evidence:** *"От търговска гледна точка - не можеш до изградиш бранд. Нещата са точно като да вземеш сергия в мол."*
*   **The Content Idea:** **Are You Building Your Brand or Your Platform's?** Speak to the fear of not truly owning their store. Building on a rigid SaaS means renting space and restricted SEO (URL structures, canonicals).
*   **The Pitch:** Next.js Server-Side Rendering gives 100% control over URL structures and rich metadata. *"Stop building your platform's domain authority instead of your own. DM me."*

### 5. The Courier Plugin Dependency Trap (Econt/Speedy)
*   **The Real Evidence:** *"стигнах до етапа с интеграцията на Еконт и каква изненада... няма как да дам 190 лв. за нещо, което го има безплатно... обещали са 'скоро' нов безплатен."*
*   **The Content Idea:** **Stop Waiting for Courier Plugins to Fix Your Checkout.** Highlight the frustration of relying on broken, expensive, or delayed 3rd-party plugins for local shipping.
*   **The Pitch:** Pitch custom Headless API integrations. Checkout flows that talk *directly* to Econt/Speedy APIs—no broken plugins. *"Tired of courier plugins breaking your checkout? Let's integrate your logistics directly via API."*
