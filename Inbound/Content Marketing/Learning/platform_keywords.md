# Platform-Specific Keywords & Hashtags

This file contains the categorized keywords and hashtags optimized for different social platforms. Use these when deploying the Copywriting skill to ensure platform-native reach.

### 💼 LinkedIn (B2B & Professional Focus)
* **Goal:** Networking, proving ROI to other business owners.
* **Hashtags:** `#EcommerceGrowth` `#ConversionRateOptimization` `#ShopifyPlus` `#Nextjs` `#TechnicalSEO`
* **Keywords to use in copy:** "ROI of headless commerce", "impact of site speed on sales"

### 🐦 X / Twitter (Tech & Fast Trends)
* **Goal:** Building authority, tech-focused discussions, fast hooks.
* **Hashtags:** `#CoreWebVitals` `#WebPerf` `#HeadlessCommerce` `#BuildInPublic` `#Nextjs`
* **Keywords to use in copy:** "fix core web vitals Shopify", "heavy images slowing down WooCommerce"

### 📱 TikTok & Instagram Reels (Visual & Broad Reach)
* **Goal:** Catching attention quickly, pain-point identification.
* **Hashtags:** `#EcommerceTips` `#ShopifyTips` `#BusinessOwner` `#WebsiteSpeed` `#CRO`
* **Keywords to use in copy:** "why is my ecommerce site so slow", "Shopify mobile conversion drops"

### 🎥 YouTube Shorts (Search & Educational)
* **Goal:** Ranking for "How-To" queries and technical tutorials.
* **Hashtags:** `#EcommerceSEO` `#ShopifyTutorial` `#WebsiteOptimization` `#NextjsTutorial`
* **Keywords to use in copy:** "headless Next.js ecommerce architecture", "ecommerce site speed optimization services"
