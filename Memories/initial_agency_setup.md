# Initial Agency System Factory Setup
**Subtitle / Short Detail:** Documentation of the foundational folder structure, initial Bulgarian E-commerce persona, and the "Free Website" campaign strategy created to launch the Next.js agency.

---

## Summary / Details

Today, we successfully laid the foundation for a scalable Next.js web development agency. Here is a breakdown of what was accomplished:

1. **Brand Foundations:** 
   - Created the `Brand Info` directory containing `about_us.md` with contact information and roles for Yeasin Arafat (Next.js Developer) and Mukitur Rahman Raju (Marketing).
   - Added a `portfolio.md` documenting successfully delivered client projects (e.g., Rackup IT, BD Feature).

2. **Market & Persona Research:**
   - Conducted deep-dive browser research into the Bulgarian e-commerce niche.
   - Developed a highly detailed persona ("Ivan The Pragmatist") who struggles with scaling, slow database queries on Open Source platforms, and fears breaking his site with plugin updates.
   - Identified Next.js as the perfect headless solution to solve his speed and international scalability issues.

3. **Campaign Strategy:**
   - Designed our first marketing campaign: offering 5 free Next.js websites to our target persona to quickly build a relevant portfolio and establish trust in the Bulgarian market.

4. **The "Factory" Structure:**
   - Organized the entire workspace into a robust Inbound/Outbound machine.
   - **Inbound:** Separated into `Content Marketing` and `Social Engagement`, each with their own dedicated folders for `Strategies`, `Learnings`, and execution history.
   - **Outbound:** Structured with `Outreach` (including a `lead_list.md`), alongside `Strategies` and `Learnings`.

5. **AI Memory System:**
   - Established the `memories` folder and defined strict rules for AI context retrieval, ensuring all future memories include a scannable Title and Subtitle to preserve processing efficiency.
