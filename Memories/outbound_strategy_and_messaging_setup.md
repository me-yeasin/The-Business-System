# Outbound Strategy and Messaging Setup
**Subtitle / Short Detail:** Documentation of the research, refined lead qualification criteria, outreach messaging pivot, and step-by-step outbound execution strategy created for the Next.js agency.

---

## Summary / Details

Today, we successfully built the outbound sales framework for our Next.js web development agency, pivoting our messaging strategy to attract high-value, established e-commerce clients. Here is a breakdown of what was accomplished:

1. **Lead Qualification Refinement:**
   *   Conducted deep-dive research into top B2B sales and agency communities (Reddit, IndieHackers, LinkedIn).
   *   Created `Outbound/Learnings/lead_qualification_criteria.md` to establish quality filters:
       *   **Green Flags:** Prospects showing "Money in Motion" (active ad spend, hiring marketing roles), High LTV, failing Google Core Web Vitals, and camera-willingness on Zoom.
       *   **Red Flags/Disqualifiers:** Startups with zero revenue ("Wantrepreneurs"), premature price negotiations, and clients blaming previous developers.

2. **Outreach Messaging Pivot:**
   *   Identified that offering a completely "free website" acts as a red flag for established businesses, as they associate it with scams, low quality, or hidden hosting traps.
   *   Created `Outbound/Learnings/outreach_messaging_lessons.md` detailing the transition to the **"Speculative Mockup"** or **"Loom Video Audit"** approach.
   *   Documented high-performing templates that ask a simple, low-friction "Yes/No" question (e.g., *"We redesigned one of your pages. Should I send it over?"*).

3. **Outbound Execution Strategy:**
   *   Drafted `Outbound/Strategies/outbound_execution_strategy.md`, providing a step-by-step playbook for finding, selecting, pitching, and qualifying prospects.

4. **Campaign Strategy Update:**
   *   Modified `Brand Info/campaigns/free_website_offer.md` to align with these learnings, changing the core campaign from a "Free Website Offer" to a "5 Free Performance Mockups" strategy.

5. **Git Version Control:**
   *   Staged and committed all new files to the Git repository.
