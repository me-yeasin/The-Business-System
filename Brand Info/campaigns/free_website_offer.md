## Campaign Goal
- Secure **5 high-paying retainer clients** by first delivering 5 speculative redesigns/mockups.

## Strategy
- **Opening Offer:** Instead of pitching a completely free website (which triggers skepticism in established businesses), we will offer a **Free Speculative Redesign or High-Performance Mockup** of a single critical page (e.g., their slowest product page or checkout flow).
- **The Value Proposition:** This proves our Next.js expertise and speed improvements upfront without making us look cheap or desperate. We deliver the mockup/audit (often via a Loom video) to show exactly how much faster and higher-converting their site could be.
- **The Backend Conversion:** Once they see the undeniable proof of value, we transition them into a paid engagement to build out the full headless Next.js site and retain them for ongoing optimization.
